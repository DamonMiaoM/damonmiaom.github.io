About 关于我

A life insurance advisor learning to build with AI — one project at a time 一位寿险顾问，借助 AI，正在一步步建造属于自己的数字世界

Hi, I’m Damon Miao. I write about AI, technology, and thinking clearly.

I’m interested in how large language models work, how to reason carefully about complex systems, and what it means to think well in a technical world.

This site is where I share notes, essays, and projects.

Thanks to Claude Code, even without any developing experience, I’m able do create this site. It gives me a feeling of discovery something new, a new world, continent, planet.

Now I’m building up something from nowhere, and I’m going to grow it to somewhere we can meet, by destiney.
